Welcome to Pikes Peak Aikido

Aikido is a Japanese martial discipline for training the mind, body and spirit. It is an art of personal refinement where technique is not an objective but a tool of mental, physical, and spiritual growth. At the core philosophy of Aikido are two fundamental threads: a commitment to peaceful conflict resolution whenever possible and a commitment to self-improvement through practice. Pikes Peak Aikikai has been a part of the Colorado Springs martial arts scene for over 25 years. We are a non-profit dojo run cooperatively by the dojo members. Pikes Peak Aikikai is a member of the Aikido Schools of Ueshiba, an international federation of of more than 70 dojos.
