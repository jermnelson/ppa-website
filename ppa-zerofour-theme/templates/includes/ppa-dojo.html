About Pikes Peak Aikikai

Rules of the Dojo

Pikes Peak Aikikai is an active member of Aikido Schools of Ueshiba, founded and still lead by an original uchi-deci of O'Sensei, Saotome Sensi. These rules come directly from the ASU Handbook

The dojo follows traditional rules of proper conduct. Its spirit comes directly from the Founder of Aikido and it is the place of the succession of his teachings.

It is the responsibility of each student to cooperate in creating a positive atmosphere of harmony and respect.
